Colin Raffel

I am currently a Senior Research Scientist at Google Brain. I work primarily on generative models and representation learning with applications to sequential data. In 2016, I received a Ph.D. from Columbia University in Electrical Engineering with a focus on machine learning. My Ph.D. work was supervised by Daniel P. W. Ellis. I'm currently applying for a tenure-track faculty position at a research university.
